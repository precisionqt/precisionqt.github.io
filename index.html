about

Quazar Scanning Tunnelling Microscopes (STM)

  1. Ambient STMs
  2. Vacuum STMs
  3. Cryogenic variable temperature STMs

Quazar Physical Property Measurement Systems (PQMS)

  1. Cryogenic DC Electrical Conductivity System
  2. Cryogenic DC Hi-Conductivity System
  3. Cryogenic DC Hi-Resistivity System
  4. Cryogenic DC Magnetoresistance and Hall Coefficient System
  5. Cryogenic AC Magneto Susceptibility System
  6. Vibrating Sample Magnetometer

QRYSTAL Chemical Vapour Deposition System (CVD)

  1. Atmorpheric Pressure CVD
  2. Low Pressure CVD
  3. Graphene CVD
  4. MoS2 CVD
  5. MoSe2 CVD

Quazar Data Acquisition Systems (QDA)

  1. 10KHz 4 channel simultaneous Data Acquisition
  2. 10KHz 2 channel simultaneous Data Acquisition
  3. 1MHz 8 analog channel and 16 digital channel simultaneous Data Acquisition
  4. 100MHz 4 Channel Excitation, 4 Acquisition simultaneous data acquisition system
  5. 100MHz multiplexed 64 Channel Excitation, 64 Acquisition simultaneous data acquisition system

QDA Compatible Signal Conditioners

  1. Voltage Signal Conditioners
  2. Ratiometric Signal Conditioners
